True Value ends business

Massive sales debut before the business shuts its doors

After being Campbellsport’s most popular hardware seller for decades, True Value is finally going out of business for good. This is the final weekend for the beloved hardware store. At the end of the weekend the business will close.

Massive sales will debut this weekend which are expected to bring in lots of customers. Sales will reach discounts of up to fifty percent off select tools. Be sure to check it out!

The local True Value is recognized by every Campbellsport citizen as the backbone to many of their projects. It will forever be remembered for its knowledgeable and hospitable employees as well as durable tools and supplies. Go support your local business and take advantage of some sweet deals before it’s too late!

“True Value is where I bought my first hammer,” said lifelong resident Gary Mueller. “They knew me by name.”
